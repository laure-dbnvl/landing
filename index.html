What is CHOCOOLATE?

Chocolate is a product consumed by many people: it is found everywhere, and the price range is extremely wide. The problem is that cocoa producers are often not properly paid. The principle of CHOCOOLATE is therefore simple: since chocolate is appreciated by all, we propose you to learn how to make it for free, to give the largest possible part of the income to small producers!
